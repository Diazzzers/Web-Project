The story of Naruto follows the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest ninja and earning the title of Hokage, the leader of his village, the Hidden Leaf Village (Konohagakure). However, Naruto carries a burden that sets him apart from others - he is the host of the Nine-Tailed Fox, a powerful and malevolent beast that attacked the village years before his birth. As a result, Naruto faces prejudice and loneliness from his fellow villagers who see him as the embodiment of the danger that the Nine-Tails represents.
